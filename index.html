ABOUT ME

After completing college with a degree in Psychology focused on pre-med, I pursued an accelerated Medical Assistant training program and secured a job at a hospital. However, I soon realized my true passion lay in utilizing my critical thinking and problem-solving skills in a career better suited for them. My interest in software development was piqued upon witnessing a friend's projects at Nashville Software School. To begin my education, I used free online resources such as freeCodeCamp and codeCademy. I was accepted into Cohort 58 of Nashville Software School in early 2022, and the ensuing six months provided me with the most enriching education experience of my life. Now, I am a proficient full-stack software developer, and I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field.
